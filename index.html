We provide a nurturing environment for children to grow and learn.

Welcome to Kunya Primary School

Kunya Primary School is dedicated to providing a nurturing and inclusive environment where every child can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. We serve students from Grades 1 through 6, focusing on holistic development and lifelong learning.

Our school hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Please ensure students arrive on time to maximize learning opportunities.

School Life & Activities

At Kunya Primary, we believe learning extends beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular activities include:

  • Sports clubs: football, athletics, and volleyball
  • Music and drama club
  • Science and robotics club
  • Environmental conservation projects
  • Annual school trips and cultural events

Information for Parents

  • School Hours: Students should arrive by 7:45 AM. School starts at 8:00 AM sharp. Pick-up is at 3:00 PM.
  • Supply Lists: Available at the school office and on our website.
  • Student Handbook: Contains school policies, code of conduct, and academic expectations. Download PDF
  • Dress Code: Students must wear the official school uniform daily.
  • Parent Portal: Sign up for the PowerSchool Parent Portal to track your child's progress.
